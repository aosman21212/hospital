Hospital Management System

A complete, all-in-one healthcare module for Odoo 19 — Patients · Appointments · Lab Tests · Pharmacy · Billing

Odoo 19 v 1.0.0 Healthcare Free & Open Source LGPL-3
Everything your hospital needs
Purpose-built for Odoo 19 — no extra dependencies beyond the standard accounting & product modules.
👤

Patient Management

Full patient records with photo, date of birth, blood group, contact info and colour-coded tags (Urgent, Diabetic, Elderly…). Auto-generated reference numbers.

📅

Appointment Scheduling

Lifecycle workflow: Draft → Confirmed → In Progress → Done. Calendar view for daily/weekly scheduling. Priority levels, notes, and chatter.

🧪

Lab Tests & Results

Order tests, collect samples, track analysis. Upload X-rays, CT scans, ECG and unlimited result images. Normal/High/Low/Critical result status badges.

🖼️

Medical Image Gallery

Primary image shown large (up to 800×600). Additional images gallery with drag-and-drop reordering, filenames and notes. Thumbnails for quick review.

💊

Pharmacy & Prescriptions

Issue prescriptions with medicines, dosage, route (oral/IV/topical/…) and frequency (BD/TDS/PRN/STAT…). Workflow: Draft → Confirmed → Dispensed.

🧾

Accounting Integration

Generate customer invoices directly from completed appointments with one click. Links to existing Odoo invoicing, payment states and journal entries.

🔐

Role-Based Access Control

Three security groups — Read Only, User, Manager — with per-company record rules for full multi-company support.

📄

PDF Reports

Printable Appointment Sheet (with service lines and totals) and Patient Card. QWeb templates ready for customisation.

💬

Mail, Activities & Followers

Full Odoo chatter on patients, appointments, lab tests and prescriptions. Schedule activities, log notes and track every change automatically.

Installation
  1. Copy the hospital_management folder into your Odoo add-ons directory.
  2. Restart the Odoo server.
  3. In the Apps menu, search for "Hospital Management" and click Install.
  4. The Hospital top-level menu appears automatically.
  5. Go to Configuration → Lab Test Types to set up your lab catalogue.
  6. Assign users to the Hospital User or Hospital Manager group in Settings → Users.
